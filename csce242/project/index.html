Formula One

Formula One (F1) is the pinnacle of international auto racing, featuring cutting-edge technology and some of the fastest cars in the world reaching upwards of over 200 MPH. The F1 World Championship, organized by the FIA, has been a the highest challenge in motorsports since 1950, stunning fans with its blend of speed, strategy, and excitement.
